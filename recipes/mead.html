Valhalla's original Mead


Description

Brewed by the Valkyries only for the true warriors

Ingredients

  • 1 litre of your enemies' blood
  • 1 litre of honey
  • 1 tbspn of Valikirian tears
  • A pinch of love
  • A fistful of dynamite
  • 1 medium size pickle

Steps

  1. Mix blood with love in a wooden bowl
  2. Spread honey all over your forehead
  3. Put a pickle in your left ear (sorry Vincent)
  4. Add a fistful of dynamite and Valkirian tears to a bowl whilst stirring anti-clockwise
  5. Once the solution becomes golden keep stirring until you recite the Leo Tolstoy's 'War and Peace' backwards
  6. Pour it out all over the floor and go collect your mead from the nearest Valhalla souvenir store
